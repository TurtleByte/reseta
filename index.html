About Us

Culture, Prescribed.

A Rooted Identity

Reseta will always put the local scene first. We believe that contemporary Filipino artisans have much to offer in both the things they create and the things they adore and admire. It is in our personal interest for more to be aware of these up-and-coming influences and how they absorb the world around them.

Integrity Above All

There's no need to worry about shady business practices or back door data collection or whatever you can think of. Our only goal is to ensure that you get your prescription in pristine condition. If you aren't satisfied with your box or if you have a problem with your Reseta, contact us and we'll make sure to make it right.

Cultivating A Voice

There are underappreciated creators in every form of medium. There are always fans and fellow artisans that wish that the ones they look up to are also recognized by many; to serve as an homage and promotion as a thank you and physical manifestation of their appreciation. This platform helps give those small, unrecognized creators an amplifier.

Sustaining New Connections

We aim to connect the parts of the world that never would've come together by pure random chance. In a world of infinitely many connections, some can only last milliseconds if any time at all. By giving recognition to some, we can create links between people and cultures that will remain for years to come.

A Box That Fits

We make sure the everything about the physical box that houses each Reseta's contents are elegant, sturdy, and simplistic. Large enough to serve more than a vehicle to get from us to you but also as a lounge for objects you hold valuable.

Upwards and Outwards

We believe in continuous improvement. In each and every day, we strive towards making our product, our business, our relationships, and our processes better. This allows us to always ensure that we're operating at our peak and finding room to grow and expand.
